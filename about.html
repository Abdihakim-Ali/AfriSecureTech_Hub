The Company

AfriSecureTech Hub is re-imagining network operations and security by creating a new industry standard for deploying, monitoring, and securing network infrastructures.

With the rapid growth of digital transformation, the explosion of connected devices, and the shortage of experienced cybersecurity professionals, building and maintaining secure network infrastructures has become exceedingly complex and expensive.

As companies grow and expand their digital footprints, visibility into their network systems decreases dramatically. Configuration mistakes occur more frequently and go unnoticed longer, causing brand-damaging data breaches, performance degradation, costly production outages, and ballooning operational costs.

This is why we built AfriSecureTech Hub. To prevent these problems by empowering companies to expertly handle every step of the cybersecurity and network management lifecycle in-house.

About us

AfriSecureTech Hub was founded by engineers, for engineers. We are former network architects and cybersecurity consultants aiming to empower anyone technical to effortlessly build and operate secure, resilient network infrastructures.

We know how much time, energy, and budget goes into ensuring your network operations run smoothly because we've been there ourselves. We want to help you put those valuable resources into your core business instead.

We're relentless about improving our platform from both a technical and UX perspective. User feedback factors into everything we do. We're always happy to hear from our users--so don't hesitate to reach out!

We're proud of our team's diversity and seek to improve DEI in the cybersecurity and networking space more broadly.
